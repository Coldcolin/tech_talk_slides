Your Roadmap to Success

1

Learn the Fundamentals

Master HTML, CSS, JavaScript. Use free resources like freeCodeCamp, Codecademy, or The Odin Project. Dedicate 1-2 hours daily.
2

Build Real Projects

Create a personal website, portfolio, calculator app, or weather app. Start simple, then add complexity. Show your work on GitHub.
3

Join Communities

Participate in coding clubs, hackathons, open-source projects. Connect with other developers on Twitter, Discord, and local meetups.
4

Stay Consistent

Progress compounds over time. Code every day, even if just for 30 minutes. Track your learning journey and celebrate small wins.
